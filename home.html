Welcome

We are a recycling program of sorts where you can get rid of your soon to be expired and unused food by posting "sale like" ads and giving people an oportunity to come pick it up free of charge. some other options we offer instead of people driving to your place to get the food, you can check out our donation locations page and find the closest food bank or non-profit location for you to drop off your goods. If donating was not something you had in mind then FEAR NOT! we also have a page where you can type in the ingredients you have left and we will show you all the recipes you can make that contain your leftover ingredients! One final option we have is a community event board where one can post upcoming events such as; "Boy Scout/Girl Scout fundraisers or food drives" or "Church canned goods drive" exc.. exc..
